ADMISSIONS POLICY

A candidate seeking admission in any of the program shall apply online or download on prescribed application form available on www.hcn.edu.pk. The candidate shall submit the application form along with the attested photocopies of all documents as mentioned in the application form. If any document / information provided by the candidate were found false/fake or fabricated at any stage of the course, the admission will be cancelled. Incomplete applications will not be entertained.
