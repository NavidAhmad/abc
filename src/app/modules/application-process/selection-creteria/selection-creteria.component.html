SELECTION CRITERIA

Candidate applying for admission shall fulfill the following criteria for admission in Generic BS Nursing Degree Program as:
  • Metric with Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics) or O Level Certificate accompanied by an equivalency Certificate from Inter Board Committee of Chairman (IBCC).
  • Intermediate (Pre-Medical) with at least 50% Marks A- Level Certificate accompanied by
  • Both male and female can apply of age limit from 17 to 25 years.
  • Entrance test
  • Interview
Candidate will be selected by calculating the percentage marks of the following as:
1 Adjusted marks obtained in FSc or Equivalent Examination 50%
2 Entry Test Marks 40%
3 SSC Marks 10%
