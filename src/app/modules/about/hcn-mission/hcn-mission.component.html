Mission

The mission of the Himalaya College of Nursing is to educate students, advance knowledge, and provide service to the University, the profession, and the community through excellence in the discovery, application, integration, and dissemination of knowledge. The College emphasizes health promotion needs of individuals, families, groups, and communities. We aim to provide an affordable education of high quality and to focus scholarship and public service on a global vision of health promotion. Our mission is operational in local, regional, national and global initiatives.
