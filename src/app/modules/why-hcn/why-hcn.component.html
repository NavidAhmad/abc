Why Himalaya College of Nursing?

Selecting where you will study for your chosen career is one of the most transforming decisions of your life. You would like to choose a course with a high international reputation and standards that would get the best out of your God given talents. At HCN our aim is to take the best candidates into our program and give them experience and training necessary to make deference to all they treat and in so doing, we hope they will gain that empathy that will make them the nurses our world needs. HCN is dedicated to education the next generation of leaders in nursing. Comprehensive facilities, and one of the best faculties in the region, provide students with a supportive environment to acquire a scientific foundation and develop the skills, attitudes, knowledge and commitments, necessary to perform clinical and research activities at the highest level. Our commitment to excellence and collaboration across scientific and nursing disciplines creates an optimal atmosphere for learning.
