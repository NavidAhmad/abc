MEMBERSHIP & AFFILIATION

Himalaya College of Nursing and Allied Health Sciences is affiliated with the following regulatory and governing bodies
1. Pakistan Nursing Council, Islamabad

It’s a regulatory and licensing body for the nursing profession in Pakistan. Both programs –BScN and Post RN BScN – are recognized by the council. Himalaya College of Nursing following the approved curricula for BS Nursing, Post RN, rules and regulations of PNC.

For details please visit their website: http://www.pnc.org.pk

2. Higher Education Regulatory Authority (HERA) Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Higher Education Regulatory Authority (HERA) is an autonomous body, which is mandated "to streamline, supervise and regulate functioning of the degree level private institutions in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa" under the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Registration and Functioning of Private Educational Institutions Ordinance, 2001.

For details please visit their website: http://www.herakp.gov.pk

3. Khyber Medical University (KMU) Peshawar

Khyber Medical University (KMU) Peshawar is the leading Medical University in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. KMU is the degree awarding University of the Himalaya college of Nursing and Allied Health Sciences, Mardan..

For further detail on Khyber Medical University, Peshawar please visit http://www.kmu.edu.pk

4. Health Foundation Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Peshawar

The Himalaya College of Nursing and Allied Health Sciences has signed an MoU with the DHQ hospital Mardan for Clinical Practice and Training of BSN and Post RN students. Further the MoU has been properly endorsed through Health Foundation Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Peshawar.

For further details the role and function of Health Foundation please visit http://www.kphf.gov.pk

5. Nursing Education Board Peshawar (NEBP)

Nursing Education Board Peshawar is the provincial board of nursing in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa for awarding Diploma in Nursing, Midwifery, and other specialties in nursing. Himalaya College of Nursing is the constituent and affiliated Nursing College for awarding Basic Diploma of pediatric Nursing and Community Health Nursing.

For further details please visit NEB Peshawar website as https://www.pnc.org.pk/Nursing_Examination_Boards.html
