About HCN

Himalaya College of Nursing and Allied Health Sciences Mardan is a private sector national college committed to meeting the needs and ambitions of a diverse range of students by providing challenging academic Nursing programs underpinned by international accredited qualifications, social diversity, academic excellence and entrepreneurial centered achievement. The Himalaya College of Nursing & Allied Health Sciences is registered by Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) Islamabad, Approved by the Higher Education Regulatory Authority Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (HERA KP), Recognized by Health Foundation and Health Department, and College is affiliated with Khyber Medical University, Peshawar. The purpose of the establishing Himalaya College of Nursing Mardan has an edge to be different by scaling in environment in which each Nursing student is coached,mentored,counseled and provided ample support and opportunity to discover themselves;

be in terms of Nursing Leaders or in the broader terms of applications and acceptances. High quality teaching and learning at HCN is ensured at all tiers, with an unswerving dedication to the development and application of original knowledge and also sharpen scales of analysis, creative thinking and potential to be a convincing contributors of the healthy society.

MEMBERSHIP & AFFILIATION

Himalaya College of Nursing and Allied Health Sciences is affiliated with the following regulatory and governing bodies
  • Pakistan Nursing Council, Islamabad
  • Higher Education Regulatory Authority (HERA) Khyber Pakhtunkhwa
  • Khyber Medical University (KMU) Peshawar
  • Health Foundation Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Peshawar
  • Nursing Education Board Peshawar (NEBP)
